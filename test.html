ABOUT ABA

Aba is a restaurant with a rooftop patio located in Chicago's historic Fulton Market District. It is Chef CJ Jacobson's second restaurant with Lettuce Entertain You, and it is an extension of Ēma (mother in Hebrew) in River North. Aba, meaning "father" in Hebrew, incorporates Ēma's style of cooking with a larger influence of steak, raw and chilled seafood. The beverage program is crafted by Wine Director Ryan Arnold and Mixologist Liz Pearce, showcasing Mediterranean-inspired rare wines and spirits.
